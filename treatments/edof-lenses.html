Extended Depth of Focus (EDOF) Lenses

Overview

If you lead an active lifestyle, spend time on computers, or enjoy cooking and socializing, an Extended Depth of Focus (EDOF) lens might be your ideal choice. Unlike standard lenses that focus only on one single distance, EDOF lenses create a seamless, elongated stretch of focus. This provides excellent distance vision that flows perfectly into brilliant "intermediate" vision, designed specifically for the modern world.

How They Enhance Your Lifestyle:

  • Driving and Navigation: Clear distance vision for the road, flowing seamlessly into the ability to read your car's dashboard or sat-nav.
  • Hobbies and Home: Perfect for "arm's length" tasks like working on a desktop computer, reading sheet music, or seeing the food on your plate.
  • Less Reliance on Glasses: You will enjoy a high degree of visual freedom, generally only needing light reading glasses for very small print, like a medicine bottle or a novel.

Your Next Step

During your consultation, we will discuss your daily routines and hobbies to see if the smooth, continuous vision of an EDOF lens is the perfect match for the way you live your life.
