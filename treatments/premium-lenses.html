Premium Lenses Guide

Overview

Having cataract surgery is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to completely transform how you see the world. In the past, patients were only offered one standard type of lens. Today, utilizing high-precision micro-technology, we offer a complete portfolio of "Premium Lenses." These advanced implants are designed to seamlessly integrate with your eye and significantly reduce your daily dependence on glasses.

Your Premium Options Include:

  • Toric Lenses: Custom-measured to counteract astigmatism, ensuring perfectly undistorted vision.
  • EDOF Lenses: A smooth blend of distance and intermediate vision, perfect for active, modern lifestyles and computer use.
  • Trifocal Lenses: The highest level of visual freedom, covering near, middle, and far distances.

Your Next Step

Choosing a lens can feel overwhelming, but you do not have to make the decision alone. Book a consultation, and Miss Lee will guide you through your options in plain English, helping you select the implant that best fits your eyes.
