* Name, date of birth, and contact details

## How We Use Your Information
* To arrange and manage appointments
* To provide safe and effective medical care
* To communicate with you about your care
* To maintain accurate clinical records
* To process payments and insurance claims
* To improve our services

## Legal Basis
* Provision of healthcare (UK GDPR Article 9(2)(h))
* Performance of a contract with you
* Legal obligations (medical record keeping)
* Legitimate interests (practice management)

## Sharing Your Information
Your data may be shared with:
* Private hospitals and clinics where you are treated
* Other healthcare professionals involved in your care
* Your GP (where appropriate)
* Medical insurers
* Regulatory bodies where required

## Online Booking & Doctify
If you book appointments via third-party platforms (e.g. Doctify or other online booking systems):
* Your personal data may be collected and processed by those providers
* They act as independent data controllers or processors depending on the service
* Their use of your data is governed by their own privacy policies

We ensure that any third-party providers used meet appropriate data protection standards.

## Data Security
Your information is stored securely using encrypted systems and is only accessible to authorised individuals.

## Data Retention
Medical records are retained in line with UK medico-legal guidance (typically a minimum of 7–8 years).
