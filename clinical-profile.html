{{ site.practice.honorific }} {{ site.practice.full_name }} is a UK-trained Consultant Ophthalmic Surgeon specialising in advanced cataract removal and corneal conditions. A graduate of University College London and a Fellow of the Royal College of Ophthalmologists (FRCOphth), she brings sub-specialist fellowship training from the renowned Corneoplastic Unit of Queen Victoria Hospital and Frimley Park Hospital to her practice.

By utilising state-of-the-art diagnostic imaging and high-precision micro-incision surgical technology, her practice is dedicated to achieving the highest standard of visual outcomes. Also recognised as an EndoArt® Pioneer, having performed one of the first commercial artificial-cornea transplants (covered by the BBC and Frimley Health NHS). Every procedure is delivered within a calm, reassuring and patient-focussed environment. Committed to shaping the future of her specialty, Miss Lee serves as a surgical trainer and clinical supervisor, actively mentoring and training the next generation of ophthalmic surgeons.

Miss Lee holds substantive consultant posts at Frimley Health NHS Foundation Trust. Her private practice operates across Parkside Frimley Park, Heatherwood Hospital, and Benenden Hospital, where she remains dedicated to delivering exceptional, patient-centred eye care.
