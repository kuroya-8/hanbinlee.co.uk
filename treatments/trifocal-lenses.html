Trifocal Lenses

Overview

For patients who want to reduce their reliance on glasses as much as medically possible, Trifocal lenses offer the most advanced technology available today. As the name suggests, these incredibly clever lenses split light to provide clear, focused vision across all three essential distances. While they represent a significant investment in your vision, they provide the ultimate level of independence from spectacles.

The Three Zones of Vision:

  • Near Vision: Comfortable reading for books, menus, sewing, and looking at your mobile phone.
  • Intermediate Vision: Crisp focus for computer screens, tablets, and conversation distances across a dinner table.
  • Distance Vision: Sharp clarity for driving, watching television, and enjoying beautiful landscapes.

Your Next Step

If your goal is maximum independence from your glasses, book an assessment. We will take highly precise measurements of your eyes to confirm if you are a healthy candidate for this remarkable, life-changing technology.
