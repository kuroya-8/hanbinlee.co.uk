**Callout** — tinted notice block, or the practice's signature italic teal-bar pull quote.

```jsx
<Callout variant="quote">Advancing surgical excellence through digital integration and precision patient care.</Callout>
<Callout tone="info" title="Insured patients">Have your authorisation code ready before booking.</Callout>
```

`variant="quote"` for the mission quote; `variant="block"` (default) with `tone` `info|success|warning|danger`, optional `title` and `icon`.
