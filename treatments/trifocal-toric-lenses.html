Trifocal Toric Lenses

Overview

In the past, patients with astigmatism had to compromise—they could not easily enjoy the benefits of advanced multifocal lenses. Today, Trifocal Toric lenses combine the best of both worlds into one remarkable, all-in-one implant. They correct the irregular "rugby ball" shape of your eye while simultaneously splitting light to provide clear vision across all distances.

Comprehensive Visual Freedom:

  • Astigmatism Correction: Custom-built to smooth out your eye's unique curvature.
  • Distance, Middle, and Near: Enjoy reading, computer work, and driving, all with significantly reduced reliance on glasses.
  • A Bespoke Solution: This is a highly personalized implant, tailored specifically to your eye's exact microscopic measurements.

Your Next Step

If you have an astigmatism but still want the freedom to live an active life without constantly searching for your glasses, arrange a consultation. We will determine if this state-of-the-art lens is the right match for you.
