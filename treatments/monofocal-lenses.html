Monofocal & Enhanced Lenses

Overview

Standard Monofocal lenses are brilliant at providing incredibly sharp, high-definition vision at one specific distance—usually set for looking far away. An Enhanced Monofocal (often called Monofocal Plus) lens takes this a step further, providing the same brilliant distance vision while gently extending your focus just enough to clearly see things at "arm's length."

Which is Right for You?

  • Standard Monofocal: The perfect choice if you want crisp vision for driving or watching TV, and are completely happy putting on reading glasses for menus, phones, and computers.
  • Enhanced Monofocal: Ideal if you want excellent distance vision plus the ability to clearly see your car's dashboard or a supermarket shelf without glasses (though you will still reach for reading glasses for a book or newspaper).

Your Next Step

We will help you weigh the benefits of both options during your consultation, ensuring your final choice perfectly supports the activities you enjoy most.
