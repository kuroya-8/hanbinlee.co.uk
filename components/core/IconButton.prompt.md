**IconButton** — compact icon-only control for toolbars, dialog close, menu toggles and social links.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Call practice" variant="secondary" shape="circle">
  <i data-lucide="phone"></i>
</IconButton>
```

Variants `primary | secondary | ghost | subtle`; sizes `sm 32 · md 40 · lg 48`; `shape="circle"` for round. Always supply `label`.
