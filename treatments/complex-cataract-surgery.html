Complex Cataract Surgery

Overview

A cataract is a natural clouding of the lens inside your eye, which gradually makes your vision blurry, dim, or faded. While standard cataract removal is a highly common procedure, some eyes require a more highly specialized approach. Complex cataract surgery simply means that your eye has unique characteristics requiring advanced surgical techniques, specialized micro-equipment, or meticulous extra planning. Miss Han Bin Lee specializes in these intricate cases, providing expert, consultant-led care for eyes that need that crucial extra level of attention.

Common Examples of Complex Cases:

  • Highly Dense (Mature) Cataracts: If a cataract has been developing for many years, it becomes very hard and requires specialized techniques to gently break it up and remove it.
  • Previous Eye Surgery or Trauma: If you have had previous procedures or past trauma to the eye, the internal structures may be more delicate.
  • Underlying Eye Conditions: Conditions like glaucoma or a history of severe inflammation require tailored surgical planning to ensure the safest possible outcome.

Your Next Step

Do not worry if you have been told your cataracts are complex. You will undergo a comprehensive, unhurried assessment to map your eye perfectly, allowing us to create a customized surgical plan that prioritizes your safety and visual recovery.
