Toric Lenses

Overview

If you have been told you have an astigmatism, it simply means the front of your eye is shaped slightly more like a rugby ball than a perfectly round football. If a standard lens is implanted into an eye with an astigmatism, your vision will still be slightly distorted or blurry without glasses. Toric lenses are the elegant, highly precise solution to this common issue.

The Benefits of Toric Correction:

  • Custom Engineering: Each lens is custom-measured to exactly counteract the unique curve of your specific eye.
  • Crisp Clarity: By neutralizing the astigmatism, the lens provides beautifully focused, high-definition vision.
  • Built-in Convenience: This astigmatism correction can be built into standard distance lenses or advanced multifocal lenses.

Your Next Step

During your detailed eye mapping, we will measure the exact degree of your astigmatism and discuss how a custom Toric lens can provide you with the absolute sharpest surgical outcome.
