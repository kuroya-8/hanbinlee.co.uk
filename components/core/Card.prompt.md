**Card** — surface container for fee panels, service cards, and content blocks.

```jsx
<Card variant="elevated" padding="lg">
  <h4>Parkside Suite, Frimley</h4>
  <p>First consultation · £250</p>
</Card>
```

Variants `elevated` (white + soft shadow, default) · `outline` · `soft` (mist) · `tint` (teal). `padding` `none|sm|md|lg`; `interactive` adds a hover lift for clickable cards.
